## 🖌 Overview  
Art Text Advanced is a specialized graphic design application focused on creating stunning text effects, logos, badges, and web graphics. It gives designers and non-designers alike the tools to produce professional-quality typographic artwork without a steep learning curve.

The material library contains hundreds of presets covering metal, wood, glass, neon, and countless other surface types. Apply them to any text or shape and adjust lighting, reflection, and texture parameters to achieve exactly the look you want.

3D extrusion, perspective transformations, and depth controls let you build dimensional text that stands out in any context — from app icons and social media graphics to signage and merchandise mockups.

Export options cover every use case: high-resolution PNG with transparency, SVG for scalable use, and web-optimized formats for digital publishing.

Art Text Advanced is the creative tool for designers, marketers, and content creators who want beautiful typographic graphics without the complexity of a full design suite.

## 🚀 Why Choose Art Text Advanced?

Art Text Advanced represents the perfect balance between powerful functionality and elegant simplicity. Built specifically for Apple Silicon, this application takes full advantage of cutting-edge technologies including Metal graphics acceleration, Grand Central Dispatch for multi-threading, and native components that ensure buttery-smooth performance even on older hardware.

The application has been meticulously optimized for both Intel-based systems and the latest Apple Silicon chips, including M1, M2, M3, and M4 processors. Whether you are running older hardware or the latest devices, you will experience the same level of responsiveness and efficiency.

What truly sets Art Text Advanced apart is its commitment to user privacy and data security. All processing happens locally on your device, with no data ever sent to external servers. Your documents and work remain completely private and under your control.

Regular updates bring not just bug fixes, but meaningful feature enhancements based on real user feedback. The development team actively listens to the community and implements requested improvements, ensuring the software evolves alongside your needs.

The application seamlessly integrates with your existing workflow, supporting drag-and-drop functionality, Quick Look previews, Spotlight searching, and even Shortcuts automation for power users.
